About This Project

Hi, I’m Nino, and I’m currently applying to the MS&E program. During the application process, I discovered that while the program offers immense flexibility, it also poses challenges in helping students organically connect with their peers.

This realization inspired me to create this platform. By curating resources, events, and opportunities, my goal is to make it easier for MS&E students to find their community, collaborate, and thrive.

Housing Guide

Based on insights from recent alumni, Rains Houses stands out as an excellent option for building strong connections with fellow graduate students.

Why?

  • Community-Oriented: Alumni praise Rains for fostering a vibrant social atmosphere, ideal for meeting peers across programs.
  • Convenient Location: Located near campus facilities, it provides easy access to classes and events.

Key Features

Rains Houses offer a range of features tailored to graduate student life:

  • Single and shared apartments with modern amenities
  • Frequent social events and activities organized by the housing community
  • Proximity to dining options and public transport
