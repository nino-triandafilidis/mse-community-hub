About This Project

At the heart of this project is a simple yet meaningful mission: to help MS&E students forge authentic connections that enhance their academic and personal journeys. While the program’s flexibility empowers students to tailor their experience, it can also create challenges in organically connecting with peers.

Inspired by conversations with current students and recent alumni, we’ve crafted this platform to bridge those gaps. By curating resources, events, and opportunities, we aim to make it easier for students to find their community, collaborate, and thrive together. Whether you’re new to campus or looking to expand your network, this hub serves as your starting point.

Housing Guide

Based on insights from recent alumni, Rains Houses stands out as an great option for building strong connections with fellow graduate students.

Why Rains Houses?

  • Community-Oriented: Alumni praise Rains for fostering a vibrant social atmosphere, ideal for meeting peers across programs.
  • Convenient Location: Located near campus facilities, it provides easy access to classes and events.

Key Features

Rains Houses offer a range of features tailored to graduate student life:

  • Single and shared apartments with modern amenities
  • Frequent social events and activities organized by the housing community
  • Proximity to dining options and public transport
